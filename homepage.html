WELCOME TO HORIZON ACADEMY

SCHOOL AND COMMUNITY

Horizon Academy is a public, suburban high school that opened in August, 1992. The school serves the educational needs of approximately 2,300 students, grades nine through twelve. Approximately 75% of graduates enroll in colleges and universities. Horizon Academy is a comprehensive four-year high school offering interesting and unique courses. A strong emphasis is placed on helping students to improve their study habits and study skills. Both students and staff make use of the extensive state of the art computer technology available at Horizon Academy.
